My AHS Hero: Evan Peters

Interests | Hero

Evan Peters is my favorite actor from American Horror Story. He is known for playing many very different and challenging characters. I admire him because he can show deep emotions and fully transform into each role he plays.

Murder House
He played Tate Langdon, a complex and emotionally troubled character.
Asylum
He portrayed Kit Walker, a kind man who is wrongly accused of a serious crime.
Hotel
He played James Patrick March, a dark and terrifying hotel owner.
