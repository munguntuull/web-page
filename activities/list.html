  3. What are special entity characters?
    • Special entity characters are reserved characters in HTML that have specific meanings, such as < for less than and > for greater than.
  4. What are phrase tags?
    • Phrase tags are HTML tags used to define the meaning and structure of text, such as <em> for emphasis and <strong> for strong importance.
  5. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?
    • OL (Ordered List) tags are used for lists where the order matters, while UL (Unordered List) tags are used for lists where the order does not matter.
  6. What is a DL?
    • A DL (Definition List) is used to define terms and their descriptions in a list format.
